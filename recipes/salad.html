Description

This fresh garden salad is a healthy mix of crisp vegetables and a simple olive oil dressing. Perfect for any meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and carrots.
  3. Mix all vegetables in a bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Add salt and pepper, toss well, and serve immediately.
