Description

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and lots of cheese. It's the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, and Italian herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles and prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, let cool, and serve hot.
