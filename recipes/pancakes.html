Description

This fluffy pancake recipe is perfect for breakfast or brunch. Serve with syrup, fruits, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients.
  3. Combine and mix.
  4. Cook on a skillet.
